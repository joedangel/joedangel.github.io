Privacy Policy

Our app uses Firebase Analytics, a service provided by Google, to collect and analyze usage data in order to improve the app experience. Firebase Analytics helps us understand how users interact with our app, including actions such as page views, session durations, and engagement with specific features. This data is used in an aggregated, anonymous manner to enhance the functionality and user experience of our app. We do not collect personally identifiable information through Firebase Analytics, and no personal data is shared with third parties for advertising purposes. For more details about Firebase’s data privacy practices, please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy.
